How to Visit the Museum

Follow these steps to enjoy our cyber museum experience:

1

Download VRChat from google play store

Visit VRChat google play store VRChat and press the install button.

2

Log In with google accounts

Once your account created, log in using your google account to access the museum platform.

3

Look up Museum of Cybersecurity

Press the search icon, and look up "Museum of Cybersecurity", and then press the launch world button.

Important Notes:

  • For the best experience, ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • If you need assistance, contact our support at info@cyberity.foundation
